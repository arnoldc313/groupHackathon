Beaches

Solution

For the solution for cleaning beaches we need people to volunteer to help clean beaches and pollution so people won't become sick and have a virus and also for animals to not get sick from polluted waste, that can really hurt the ecosystem badly from trash . People can log on to our website and schedule a clean up for a beach on a specific day. Other people may sign up to help that beach clean up as well. Funded by donations and partners, materials such as trash bags and gloves will be provided.

Also another solution would be not to throw away trash on beaches and instead have more trash cans on the beach and have those trash cans taken out immediately so beaches don't have any issues. There will be a point system that will earn you gift cards, T- shirts,clothes and other rewards as well for helping, to incentivize volunteers to help out. Points will be rewarded based on how big the beach is. Bonus points will be given out when there are more people helping to clean the beach, which can encourage people to invite

their friends to help as well. The rewards are also funded through donors and our partners. Another solution would be to help clean up rivers that's connected to beaches so trash will not be floating the streams and animal populated areas, that could ruin ecosystems and eventually hurt a lot of animals on the way.
